# About Me

I am a Medical Imaging AI Researcher currently pursuing a PhD at the **Université de Rennes (France)**, where I develop AI-driven methods for **planning and guidance in ventricular tachycardia radiotherapy**.

My work focuses on the complete clinical imaging workflow, from **medical image acquisition and preprocessing** to **deep learning model development and evaluation**.

I enjoy building reproducible, modular and clinically-oriented AI solutions that bridge **medical imaging research** and **real-world healthcare applications**.
